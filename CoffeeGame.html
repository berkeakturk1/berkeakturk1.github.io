Untitled Coffee Brewing Game

New Updates!

I've added a coffee shop interior so now customers will come in and out of your coffee shop. Now you can also brew esperssos and espresso based drinks aswell. I've also added a game menu in this edition of ht game. Stay tuned for more updates.

In this coffee game, you have to serve customers the coffee they want and match their orders. As you earn more money, you can buy more equipment to upgrade your coffee joint. You can use the guide and recipe book to help you brew and serve different kinds of coffee. But be careful, the game gets more chaotic as you progress.

At the moment the game is very skeletal But it will be my main focus during the summer months. Currently there isn't a set timeline or a timeframe when I can say the game will finish, However I will be working on the game and will try to finish it as soon as possible.

Your goal is to brew the coffee that matches the customer’s order as quickly as possible. You have to pay attention to the type of coffee, the amount of milk, sugar, and other ingredients. If you don’t deliver the coffee within the time limit, the customer will lose their patience and walk away. You will miss the opportunity to earn money and increase your reputation.
